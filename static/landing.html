HISTORIA

The Peruvian College was born in 1962 at the initiative of Bishop Mons. Orazio Ferruccio Ceol, a Franciscan from Trento (Italy); who, after 19 years of missionary apostolate in China, had accepted the invitation of Pope Pius XII to come to Peru as a missionary of the Chinese Community. Monsignor Ferruccio, convinced of the important role that Christian education has in the evangelizing task, proposed to found a Catholic educational center for the children and young people of the Chinese Community, with the certainty of being able to reach their parents in this way and bring them the Gospel. The school would also create favorable conditions for the of the best of the two cultural worlds, the Peruvian and the Chinese; contributing in this way to the creation of a new society, richer for being pluralistic and intercultural, and more in keeping with the new times of a globalized world. ¿Qué es?
El Ideario es un marco doctrinal o de principios
sobre el que se asientan el Proyecto
Educativo y la acción de la tarea educativa de toda
institución. Principios sobre el tipo de
hombre y de sociedad que se quiere formar y que quieren
ser una filosofía educativa, un
referente que da carácter propio y distintivo a la
institución, imprimiéndole una identidad. De
este modo, el ideario da unidad de intención a la historia educativa de la institución.
